Ultimate Video Translator Chrome Extension

Ultimate Video Translator is a real time video translator for NRK TV , Youtube and other websites.

Download link:
Chrome Web Store
How to use:

Choose your language and click on Start button then double-click or (Ctrl-key and left-click) on any element to translate it. Do it again to show the original element.

On Youtube: Choose your language and click on Translate Youtube Caption.

Why Ultimate Video Translator?

Basically, i wrote this tool because i am learning norwegian language and watching NRK TV with arabic subtitle is so important to achieve my norwegian language goal.
